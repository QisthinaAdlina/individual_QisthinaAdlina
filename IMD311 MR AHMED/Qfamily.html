Family history is very important to an individual. By knowing where I came from, and can have a better perspective of my life. Having a clear understanding of your family background allows me to better appreciate the things that would typically take for granted. The house, the car, and the average clothing may look better when one sees the sacrifices my family has made. They will see that their family has worked very hard just so my family can experience the better things in life. A person's roots and origin are one of the most important things to explore. It alone can bring me closer to self-discovery.
